Vejledning og skabelon til at løse opgaven
Den samlede eksaminationstid for assignment 1-4 er fem timer. Besvarelsen bedømmes som en helhed ud fra de faglige mål for niveauet. Der lægges vægt på beherskelsen af det engelske sprog, forståelse af forlægget og færdighed i skriftlig fremstilling på engelsk.

Det anbefales, at du skriver din besvarelse i skabelonen, som ligger under Template i menuen til venstre. Besvarelsen afleveres i ét dokument med opgaverne i rækkefølgen 1-4.

Answer 4A or 4B



Assignment 4A – Fiction


Write an analytical essay (900-1200 words) in which you analyse and interpret Kate London’s short story “We Do Not Deal in Guilt or Blame”.

Part of your essay must focus on the narrator’s development. In addition your essay must include an analysis of the style of writing in lines 1-11.

Your essay must include references to the short story.

Material
Kate London, “We Do Not Deal in Guilt or Blame”, 2019.



Assignment 4B – Non-fiction


Write an analytical essay (900-1200 words) in which you analyse Sam Anderson’s article “The Truth About Cocoons” and discuss whether a pandemic can have positive consequences.

Part of your essay must focus on how the writer engages his reader. In addition your essay must include an analysis of the style of writing in lines 1-14.

Your essay must include references to:
  • Sam Anderson’s article
  • Video: “Effects of Covid-19”

Materials
Sam Anderson. “The Truth About Cocoons”, 2020

Vox Pops International. “Effects of Covid-19”, a YouTube video, 2020
