Language functions

A simple way to categorize the functions of languge is into three categories:

  1. Informative language function
    Essentially, the communication of information.
  2. Expressive language function
    Reports feelings or attitudes of the writer (or speaker), or of the subject, or evokes feelings in the reader (or listener)
  3. Directive language function
    Language used for the purpose of causing (or preventing) overt action.
